Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how EMWIKI ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you authenticate with our service using Roblox OAuth 2.0, we collect:

  • Roblox User ID: Your unique Roblox identifier
  • Username: Your Roblox username
  • Display Name: Your Roblox display name
  • Avatar URL: Link to your Roblox avatar image

We also collect:

  • Session Information: Authentication tokens to keep you logged in
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our service (wishlist items, preferences, etc.)
  • Technical Data: IP address (for rate limiting), browser type, and device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate and identify you on our platform
  • Provide personalized features (wishlists, favorites, preferences)
  • Display your profile information (username, avatar)
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Prevent abuse and ensure security
  • Communicate with you about service updates

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Cloudflare D1: For user accounts and session data
  • Cloudflare KV: For preferences and cache data
  • Browser Local Storage: For client-side preferences (optional)

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure session token management
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Regular security updates

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Roblox: We use Roblox OAuth 2.0 for authentication (governed by Roblox's privacy policy)
  • Cloudflare: Our hosting and infrastructure provider
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Correct: Update your information (automatically synced from Roblox)
  • Opt-Out: Stop using our service at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us or simply log out and stop using the service.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use:

  • Session Tokens: Stored in browser localStorage to keep you logged in
  • Preferences: Stored locally to remember your settings
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use analytics or advertising cookies

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for Roblox users, which may include children. We:

  • Only collect necessary information for authentication
  • Do not knowingly collect additional personal information from children
  • Comply with Roblox's terms and policies regarding child safety
  • Parents/guardians can request deletion of their child's data

8. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • Active Accounts: As long as you use our service
  • Inactive Accounts: May be deleted after extended inactivity
  • Session Data: Expires after 3000 days or manual logout
  • Deletion Requests: Processed within 30 days

9. Third-Party Links

Our service may contain links to Roblox.com and other external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Please review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Website: https://emwiki.com
  • Roblox Group: Epic Minigames Community

12. Roblox Integration

This service uses Roblox OAuth 2.0 for authentication. By using our service, you also agree to:

  • Roblox Terms of Use
  • Roblox Privacy Policy
